Understanding Diabetes: More Than Just Blood Sugar

Diabetes, particularly Type 2, is characterized by high blood glucose levels, often due to insulin resistance or insufficient insulin production. Beyond fluctuating blood sugar, it can impact energy levels, metabolic function, nerve health, and overall quality of life, often leading to various complications if not well-managed. Finding holistic approaches that complement conventional treatment is vital for long-term well-being.
